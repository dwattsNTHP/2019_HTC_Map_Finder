View our list of Maps, Economic Data and Project Lists by state. Find information from all 50 states and selected congressional districts and cities.

These maps and project lists depict Federal Historic Tax Credit projects that received Part 3 certifications from the National Park Service (NPS) between the years 2002 through 2018.
