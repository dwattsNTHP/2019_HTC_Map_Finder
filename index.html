Federal Historic Tax Credit Maps and Economic Impact Data | 2001 - 2020

Knowing where historic tax credit projects are occurring in your state is valuable information to share with your elected representatives. Click on a state to access a map indicating where historic tax credit projects are located.

These maps depict federal historic tax credit projects that received Part 3 certifications from the National Park Service (NPS) between the years 2001 through 2020. Included with the maps is data indicating the economic impact federal historic tax credits are having statewide as well as a list of completed rehabilitation projects since 2001.

Additional maps, including maps of certain Congressional Districts, will be added as they are completed. Please return to this page frequently for more information about how federal historic tax credits are benefitting your community.
